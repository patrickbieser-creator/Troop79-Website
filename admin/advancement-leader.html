Scoutbook Export

Generate the pipe-delimited CSV Scoutbook accepts. Uses BSA Member IDs from each scout’s record and Advancement IDs from the lookup tables. Rows missing a BSA ID are flagged and excluded.

Export rule: Internal scout_id is the system identity. The nullable bsa_member_id is used only for Scoutbook output. Rows without a BSA Member ID stay in the system but are excluded from the CSV and flagged here for follow-up.
