v0.2 April 2, 2026 Polish

UI Refinements

  • “More This Week” story cards now render with full visual separation — white background, 1px border on all sides, 3px top accent border (turns forest green on hover), and 18px gaps between cards. Previous: flush grid with 1px hairline dividers only. Cards now read as distinct, hoverable units.
  • Navigation: Added “This Week’s Meeting” as the second nav item, between Home and News Archive. Reflects the troop’s primary weekly use case — quick access to meeting agendas.
  • Tertiary story list: Replaced sequential numbers with small filled forest-green dot bullets. Numbers implied ranking/priority, which was not the intent. Dots provide visual rhythm without false hierarchy.

Design Decisions — Approved This Session

  • Approved Visual reference: New York Times homepage layout — editorial grid, dominant hero, weekly rotation cadence
  • Approved Masthead treatment: Warm, light cream background with navy accents. Logo left-aligned. Not a dark header bar.
  • Approved Color palette: Deep navy (#1e3a4a) from logo as primary; forest green (#3d5a3e), warm bark (#8b6914), cream (#faf6ef), newsprint (#f4efe6)
  • Approved Typography: Playfair Display (headlines), Lora (body), Open Sans (UI/labels)
  • Approved CMS: WordPress on managed hosting (SiteGround or WP Engine, ~$15–30/month). Multiple editors via role-based access.
  • Deferred Authentication: Members login and private advancement view deferred to Phase 2
  • Deferred Advancement Tracker: Google Sheets integration deferred to Phase 3. User will share spreadsheet when closer.
  • Approved Payment: Venmo and PayPal links only — no payment gateway required

Open Items

  • Real photography: Scout event photos needed to replace gradient placeholders in story cards and hero image.
  • Advancement prototype: User has an existing advancement tracker prototype to share. Needed before building the Advancement Dashboard screen.
  • Remaining screens: This Week’s Meeting, News Archive, Events & Calendar, Event Registration, Resource Library, and About & Join are not yet prototyped.
