What Was Accomplished

Meeting Attendance (v1.4.0)

  • Roll Call per meeting: scout chips by patrol + leader chips, replace-on-save into the ledger (meeting_attendance kind) and a new leader-attendance table whose committed status is pre-built for future meeting-plan teaching signups. Admin-only — the public site provably ignores it.

  • Three years of history imported to production: 1,277 Google-Form check-in rows → 1,249 matched check-ins across 73 meetings via a tiered first-name matcher (same-date exclusion, per-name rulings, typo/nickname aliases, next-morning straggler folding). Also surfaced and flagged the leaders.is_person data quirk.

Calendar suite (v1.3.1, v1.5.0, v1.5.1)
