Spring Family Campout — Kettle Moraine State Forest

Two nights of tent camping, a Saturday hike loop through the glacial kettles, and a family potluck around the fire.

May
16
StartsFriday, May 16 · 5:30 PM
EndsSunday, May 18 · 11:00 AM
LocationKettle Moraine State Forest — Northern Unit, Group Camp 3
Cost$24 per scout · $12 per adult — paid offline, see below
Sign up

Scouts arrive Friday evening to set up camp; families are welcome to join for the Saturday activities and potluck even if they can’t stay overnight. Patrols cook their own meals per the patrol method — the grubmaster shops from the final headcount, which is why the signup deadline matters.
