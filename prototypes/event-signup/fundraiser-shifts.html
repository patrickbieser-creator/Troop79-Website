We take over the Legion Hall kitchen and serve the neighborhood pancakes, sausage, and juice from 7:30 to noon. It runs on shifts — pick the ones that fit your morning. Scouts’ hours are credited toward their summer-camp account, so the more shifts a scout works, the less camp costs the family.

No cost — hours are the currency

Free to attend & work

This is a fundraiser, not a paid event. Scouts are credited for the hours they work toward their summer-camp account. Nothing to pay, nothing to collect.
