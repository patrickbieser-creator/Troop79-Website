We’re back at Camp Long Lake for the summer capstone campout. Patrols will rotate through the waterfront (canoes and rowboats), the rifle and shotgun ranges, and an afternoon orienteering course. Saturday night is the patrol cook-off — grubmasters, start planning your menus once headcounts are in.

Scouts working on Camping, Cooking, or Rifle Shooting merit badges should tell their patrol leader — counselors will be on site Saturday.

Cost & payment

$30 per scout

Bring cash or a check payable to Troop 79 to any Monday meeting by Aug 10. We don’t collect payment online.

No slip + no payment = no trip.

  • ✎ Permission slip required. One per scout — download the Activity Consent form (PDF) and bring the signed copy to a Monday meeting.
  • 🚗 Drivers needed. We carpool from the church lot. If you can drive, say so when you sign up — every seat helps.
  • 🍳 Allergies & dietary needs — there’s a place on the signup form; grubmasters plan menus from it.
ⓘ Health forms: your annual AHMR Parts A & B on file are all this weekend outing needs. Part C is not required (event is under 72 hours).

Signups closed Tuesday, Aug 4 at 9:00 PM. Need to make a change or a late addition? Contact the Scoutmaster directly — don’t just show up Friday.
