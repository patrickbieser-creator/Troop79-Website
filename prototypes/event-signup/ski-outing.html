Sunburst Ski Outing

A full Saturday on the hill at Sunburst Winter Sports Park — skis, boards, and hot chocolate. First-timers welcome; rental and lift are covered by the one price.

Signup open 24 of 30 spots taken

Sunburst holds our group rate only until the deadline — and the rental shop needs everyone’s sizes ahead of time, which is why the signup form asks for height, weight, and shoe size for each person. Never skied? Perfect: the first hour includes a beginner lesson on the bunny hill.

Schedule for the day

  • 7:00 AM — depart from the church lot (carpool; drivers assigned that week)
  • 8:15 AM — arrive Sunburst, rental fitting & lift tickets
  • 9:00 AM — beginner lesson on the bunny hill; open ski/board for everyone else
  • 12:30 PM — lunch in the chalet (bring a bag lunch or ~$10 for the grill)
  • 4:30 PM — gear return, load up
  • ~6:30 PM — back at the church lot

What to bring

  • Snow pants and winter jacket (no jeans on the hill)
  • Waterproof gloves or mittens, warm hat or balaclava
  • Helmet — included free with rental; bring your own if you have one
  • Base layers and a change of dry clothes for the ride home
  • Bag lunch or grill money, and a water bottle

Details and resources are public — anyone with the event link can read them. Names never appear until after the family gate.

Cost & payment

$45 per scout

Covers lift ticket and full equipment rental. Bring cash or a check payable to Troop 79 to a Monday meeting by Jan 11. Adults skiing pay the same $45 — add it to your scout’s envelope.

No slip + no payment = no lift ticket.

  • ✎ Permission slip required. One per scout — grab the BSA permission slip from Resources above and bring the signed copy to a Monday meeting.
  • 🚗 Drivers needed. We carpool from the church lot at 7:00 AM sharp. If you can drive, say so when you sign up.
  • 📏 Sizes required at signup. The rental shop pre-stages equipment, so the form asks height, weight, and shoe size for every skier.
ⓘ Health forms: your annual AHMR Parts A & B on file cover this day outing. Part C is not required (under 72 hours).

Signups closed Monday, Jan 11 at 9:00 PM. The rental order went to Sunburst the next morning. Late additions can’t be guaranteed equipment — contact the Scoutmaster before doing anything else.
