Spring Family Campout — Kettle Moraine State Forest

A troop favorite returns: two nights of tent camping, a Saturday hike loop through the glacial kettles, and a family potluck around the fire.

By Committee Chair Lena Vogt · March 25, 2026

The Spring Family Campout kicks off riding season at Kettle Moraine, and this year the troop has the whole of Group Camp 3 reserved for Friday through Sunday. Scouts arrive Friday evening to set up camp; families are welcome to join for the Saturday activities and potluck even if they can’t stay overnight.

What to Bring

Standard three-season gear applies — overnight lows are forecast in the upper 40s. Patrols are responsible for their own cooking gear and food per the patrol method; families joining for the day should bring a dish to pass for Saturday’s potluck.

Scout Troop 79 is a family troop serving scouts and families in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. We are chartered through Scouts America and welcome boys and girls of all backgrounds.
