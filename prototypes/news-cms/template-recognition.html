Spring Court of Honor Recognizes 22 Scouts

Rank advancements, merit badges, and two Eagle Scout projects were celebrated in front of a packed room at St. John’s Fellowship Hall.

By Scoutmaster Mindy Stollenwerk · March 28, 2026

Google Photos

46 photos from Saturday’s ceremony, shared by parent volunteer Theresa King — the flag ceremony, rank advancements, the joint Eagle court, and family portraits.

View Full Album on Google Photos

Saturday’s Court of Honor was the troop’s largest in two years, with 22 scouts recognized across every rank from Scout to Eagle. Families packed St. John’s Fellowship Hall for a ceremony that ran just under ninety minutes — and, for the first time, closed with a joint Eagle court of honor for two scouts who completed their boards of review the same week.

Two New Eagle Scouts

Jordan T. and Sam W. were presented with the rank of Eagle Scout, capping off service projects that together logged over 340 volunteer hours — a new accessible reading garden at Hartwell Park and a storm-drain marking campaign with the Milwaukee Water Works.
